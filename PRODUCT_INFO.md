# LookBoard - Product Information

> Events. Notices. Announcements. Just look at the board!

---

## What is LookBoard?

LookBoard is a **smart digital notice board** that shows events, notices, and announcements on any TV or display screen.

**Simple explanation:** You add content → It shows on TV → People look at it!

---

## How It Works

```
┌──────────────────┐    ┌──────────────────┐    ┌──────────────────┐
│   ADMIN PANEL    │───▶│   LOOKBOARD      │───▶│   TV/DISPLAY     │
│  (You add stuff) │    │   (We process)   │    │  (They look)     │
└──────────────────┘    └──────────────────┘    └──────────────────┘
```

### Steps:
1. **Sign up** → Get your dashboard
2. **Add content** → Events, notices, announcements
3. **Customize** → Your logo, your colors
4. **Get URL** → yourname.lookboard.io/display
5. **Open on TV** → Full screen browser
6. **Done!** → Auto-updates every 60 seconds

## Core Features

### 1. Events Display
- Add unlimited events
- "LIVE NOW" indicator
- "COMING UP" section
- Event details: title, location, time

### 2. Notices
- Important announcements
- Scheduled notices
- Auto-expire old notices

### 3. Announcements
- Scrolling ticker
- Left-to-right or right-to-left
- Multiple messages rotation

### 4. Prayer Times (For Mosques)

- Location-based

- Hijri date

### 5. QR Donations (For Mosques)

- Multiple payment options
- Display on screen

### 6. Customization
- Your logo
- Your colors
- Your layout
- Your content

## Technical Specs

### What You Need
- Any device with modern browser
- Smart TV, Android TV, Fire TV
- Raspberry Pi with browser
- Computer with HDMI output
- Internet connection

### Supported Browsers
- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

### Display Requirements
- Minimum: 720p (1280x720)
- Recommended: 1080p (1920x1080)
- Supported: 4K (3840x2160)
