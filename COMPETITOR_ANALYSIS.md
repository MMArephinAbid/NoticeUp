## MOSQUE-SPECIFIC COMPETITORS

### 1. Masajid (masaj.id)
**Market Position:** Leading free mosque digital signage platform
**Users:** 5,000+ mosques worldwide

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Pricing** | FREE (completely free) |
| **Key Features** | Prayer times display, Athan reminders, Announcements, 15+ calculation methods (Ummul Qura, MWL, Kemenag, etc.) |
| **Hardware** | MasajidBox (plug-and-play), Android TV, Raspberry Pi, Windows Mini PC, Mac Mini |
| **Target** | Mosques globally |
| **Weakness** | Limited customization, no donation QR, no event management |

**Threat Level: HIGH** - Free pricing is very competitive

## KEY INSIGHTS FOR NOTICEUP

### Market Gaps to Exploit:

1. **Free competitors lack:**
   - Donation QR codes with tracking
   - Event management system
   - Multi-organization support (schools, clubs)
   - Custom branding removal
   - Analytics & reporting

2. **Paid competitors lack:**
   - Islamic calendar integration
   - Prayer time calculations
   - Mosque-specific features
   - Affordable pricing for non-profits

3. **Regional opportunity:**
   - No strong competitor in Bangladesh market
   - South Asian mosques underserved

### Recommended NoticeUp Positioning:

| Tier | Price | Target |
|------|-------|--------|
| **Free** | $0 | 1 screen, basic features, branded |
| **Starter** | $9/month | 1 screen, no branding, QR codes |
| **Mosque** | $29/month | 5 screens, prayer times, donations |
| **School** | $29/month | 5 screens, events, announcements |
| **Pro** | $49/month | 10 screens, analytics, priority support |

### Competitive Advantages to Build:

1. ✅ Universal (Mosque + School + Institution)
2. ✅ Donation QR with real-time tracking
3. ✅ Hijri + Gregorian calendar
4. ✅ Bangla language support
5. ✅ Local Bangladesh payment (bKash, Nagad)
6. ✅ Affordable regional pricing
7. ✅ Simple 5th-grader-friendly UI
