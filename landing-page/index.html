FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Do I need special hardware?

No! Any device with a web browser works — Smart TV, Android TV, Fire Stick, Chromecast, Raspberry Pi, or a simple computer connected to a monitor. If it can open a website, it can run NoticeUp.

How hard is it to set up?

Super easy! If you can use WhatsApp or Facebook, you can use NoticeUp. Our dashboard is designed to be simple. Most users are live in under 5 minutes with no technical help needed.

Can I update from my phone?

Absolutely! Our dashboard works on any device — phone, tablet, or computer. Update your notices from home, from work, or even while traveling. Changes appear on your displays within 60 seconds.

Is NoticeUp suitable for mosques?

Yes! We built NoticeUp with mosques as our primary focus. Features include automatic prayer times based on location, Hijri calendar date, donation QR codes, and right-to-left text support for Arabic. Over 50 mosques already use NoticeUp.

What happens if I need help?

We're here for you! Free plan users get community support. Paid plans include priority email support with typical response times under 4 hours. Enterprise customers get a dedicated account manager and phone support.

Can I try before paying?

Yes! Our Free plan is free forever — no credit card required, no time limit. Paid plans come with a 14-day free trial, and we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.

What if my internet goes down?

NoticeUp caches your content locally. If internet disconnects temporarily, your display continues showing the last known content. Once connection restores, it automatically syncs with the latest updates.
